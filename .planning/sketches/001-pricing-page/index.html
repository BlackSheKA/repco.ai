Frequently asked

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Annual gets prorated refund on downgrade.

What happens if I run out of credits?

Monitoring pauses until next month's grant or until you buy a top-up credit pack. No surprise charges.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. Free plan is forever-free, no card required. You'll only need a card to upgrade.

Why can't free users send messages?

Free is for monitoring only — see who's asking for what you sell. Outreach is a paid feature because it carries account-safety risk we manage carefully.
