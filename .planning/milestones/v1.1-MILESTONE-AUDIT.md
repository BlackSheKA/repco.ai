### Gap (medium): `health_status` / `cooldown_until` enforcement

`worker.ts` writes `social_accounts.health_status='warning'` on `security_checkpoint` / `session_expired` (line 614) and `cooldown_until` on `weekly_limit_reached` (line 629). The prescreen cron also writes `health_status='warning'` on checkpoint. **Neither column is read as an execution gate** — `executeAction` proceeds past the account fetch (line 73) without consulting `health_status` or `cooldown_until`, and the `claim_action` RPC (migration 00006) does not join `social_accounts` for these fields.

Impact: An account flipped to `warning` will continue executing approved actions until manually remediated. Affects LNKD-02 (DM/Follow failure feedback loop) and LNKD-06 (prescreen quarantine intent).

Recommended fix: add a guard in `worker.ts` immediately after the account fetch (~line 86, parallel to the active-hours check) failing early on `health_status in ('warning','banned')` or `cooldown_until > now()`.
